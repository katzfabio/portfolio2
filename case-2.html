CLIENT: Joey Kidney - Stay you

GOAL: To create an intro for Joey kidney’s YouTube channel that translates his branding. His channel is about creating a safe place for teenagers to come listen to him speak about topics such as mental wellness and relationships.

RATIONALE: I emphasized Joey’s warm and caring personality in the animation by giving texture and creating a hand drawn feeling to the frame-by-frame animation. By giving white space to the animation, it creates the delicate tone that Joey was hopeing for and also creates a freindly feeling. I carried out the same hand drawn effect by having Joey’s name write on as if he himself was signing the video." %} {% pattern banners/titles-for-sections heading="STYLEFRAMES" %}
